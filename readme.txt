This plugin allows you to use Payflow payment gateway with your Jigoshop store.

== Description ==

PayPal Payflow Pro Gateway is a new Jigoshop payment gateway which enables you to instantly receive PayPal payments and increase your sales by an average of 18%!
The best thing is that there are no monthly fees for Payflow Link and no setup is needed. Use almost any merchant account, bank, and processor, including PayPal and enjoy a safe method of digital money processing! Accept all major debit and credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, JCB, and Diners Club using just your merchant account. Payflow Gateway also supports L2/L3 purchase cards, prepaid cards, foreign currencies, TeleCheck, and ACH. What is more, you can add Basic or Advanced Fraud Protection Services to protect yourself against fraud risks (available for an additional free). Get started today!
Payflow Pro is a fully customizable gateway, so you can build a checkout experience as unique as your business—from language and layout to page sequence and PCI compliance options. You can add a PayPal button to help drive more sales, or use our hosted pages and offer PayPal Credit, too.
Fees and pricing
* setup fee – $99
* monthly fee – $25
* credit cards payment fee – $0.10
There’s an additional 2.5% charge for any currency conversion and a 1% charge to receive payments from another country.

Payflow is currently available for merchants residing in the United States, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand. With Payflow, you can accept credit card payments in any currency supported by your processor and accept PayPal payments from 202 countries around the globe.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload the plugin folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
1. Activate plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to 'Jigoshop/Manage Licenses' to enter license key for your plugin
1. Navigate to Jigoshop > Settings > Payment to configure the Payflow gateway settings.
= Steps for configuration of your Payflow installation =
1. Login to your Jigoshop store
1. Enable/Disable Payflow.
1. Set your "Method Title" and "Description". These options are seen on the checkout page.
1. Enter your Payflow "Partner name".
1. Enter your Payflow "Merchant login name".
1. Enter your Payflow "User login name".
1. Enter your Payflow "Password".
1. Save Changes.
1. Check if Your jigoshop currency is supported by Your PayPal Manager Account processor.
1. Recommended: make a few test payments before you go live. In live mode make a couple small live test payments, too.
= Important Note =
1. cURL is required to be installed on your server.
